The Vuforia Object Scanner is used to scan objects and provide real time feedback about recognition and tracking quality.

Instructions

  • Print the object scanning target and place the object on it
  • Start a scan and slowly move around the object
  • Stay close so that the object fills the camera view
  • The coverage dome will illuminate as you scan from different positions
  • Try to get coverage from all of the positions your app will need to detect from
  • Use the test option to evaluate your scan in different environments
  • Use the share option or a USB cable to transfer the scan to your computer
  • Upload the scan to the target manager to create a device database
